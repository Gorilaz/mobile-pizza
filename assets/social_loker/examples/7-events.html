7. Locker Events

You can use a wide range of events to add custom code. Available events:

  • Ready - fires when the locker is created and ready to use.
  • Lock - fires when content is locked.
  • Unlock - fires when content is unlocked.
  • UnlockByTimer - fires when content is unlocked by the Timer./li>
  • UnlockByClose - fires when content is unlocked by the Close Icon.
